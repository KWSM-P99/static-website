Our Culture

There are many guild out there, but this is our story.

Fun

We will help our guildmates with many quests - even the epic ones. Just tell the /gu that "VSR is up!" But regardless, we will always have fun, it's a game afterall.

Family

We are a bunch of nerds that love the world of EverQuest. We routinely get into mishief and participate in shenanigans with our closest friends. In KWSM, you are family - Kittens help other Kittens!

Good Ambassadors

We strive to be good Ambassadors for the server. This is the server we plan to play on for the long haul and we will go the extra mile to keep the play clean and rewarding.

  • 2014-2019

    Our Researchers Out

    Don't worry, our best kitten research squad is currently on the job. Once they return, we'll post their findings for all of the significant milestones for each of these years.

  • 2020

    The Great BBQ Season

    Some folks over in the "hardcore" raiding guilds cannot agree and successfully burn down each others house. Kittens and the rest of the server band together to demonstrate what a civilized & functional raid scene looks like. Kittens completes its first solo Vulak.

    Our very own Jutebox (we are all secretly Jutebox) champions in the P99 draft. Kittens mastered their first two attempts at solo Avatar of War. There were many Kittens to acquire new pixels!

Project 1999

Leveling

Kittens started as a leveling guild.

Our guild has many players starting new characters, leveling guild toons, and tons of new players. There is always someone to group with and tickly you nostalgia itch!

Adventure

You'll always have battle buddies.

Most of our players are located in the United States of America and our guilds official time being Central (CST). However, you can find players around the clock looking to start adventure and seek glory throughout Norrath! There is strength in a pack and Kittens stick together.

Community Nights

For new raiders and old.

Our community nights manifest in many ways. Our main focus is to bring in the entire family and set off on an adventure with a healthy balance of fun, reward, and a good bit of challenge. From guild arena nights to Plane of Hate crawls, you'll surely have a blast!

Epic Quests

VSR IS UP!!.

Yes, you'll probably get your Druid epic completed in Kittens. Also, likely any other class quest as well. Our guild has completed every epic quest, many times over, and there is enough experience to help you get that long-desired item into your hands.

Raiding

We've competed with the best of them.

From 2 AM Nagafen raids all the way to Vulak`Aerr, Kittens have conquered it all. There isn't a dragon, nuzzeled too deep in it's pile of gold, or a fish, too lost at sea for us to handle. Join Kittens for a guild whose raiding experience is clean, light hearted, and fun!

Fun With Friends

Pixel denial just isn't our thing.

Kittens is a family oriented guild. We know that being nice and courteous to our fellow servermates will go the distance and ensure the longevity of this server. We foster an inclusive community that seeks to help others, inside and outside of the guild.
